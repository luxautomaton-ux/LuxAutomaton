Frequently Asked Questions

Is Lux AI Studio really free?

Yes, Lux AI Studio is free to download and use. All features including local inference, agent management, and workflow automation are included at no cost.

Do I need an internet connection?

No. All AI processing happens locally on your machine. You can run the entire stack offline with no internet required.

Which AI models can I use?

Gemma 4, Qwen 2.5, Llama 3, and any other Ollama-compatible model. Download additional models through the built-in model manager.

How do I update to the latest version?

The app will automatically check for updates. You can also manually check for updates from the settings menu.
