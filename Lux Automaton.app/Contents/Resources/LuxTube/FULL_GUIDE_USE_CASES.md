# Lux Tube Studio - Full Guide and Use Cases

## What this app is for

Lux Tube Studio is your channel growth control room:

- scan winning channels,
- generate shorts and long-form plans,
- auto-tune AI settings,
- connect local HeyGen workflows,
- train your agent profile,
- install skills for repeatable execution.

## Recommended daily workflow

1. Open `AI Settings` and click `Auto Set Entire Lux Stack`.
2. Open `HeyGen Studio` and verify your local setup is connected.
3. Open `Skills + Training` and update niche, voice, goal, and CTA.
4. Open `Viral Scanner` and scan 5-15 channels in your niche.
5. Open `Shorts Lab` and generate your short-form execution plan.
6. Open `Long-form Lab` and generate one authority video plan.
7. Produce, publish, and feed performance back into next scan.

## Use Case 1 - Daily shorts engine

- Input: top channels in your niche.
- Action: scan + generate shorts plan.
- Output: 3-5 short scripts with hooks and pacing.
- Goal: maximize watch-through and save rate.

## Use Case 2 - Weekly long-form authority

- Input: scanner trends and audience pain points.
- Action: generate long-form plan.
- Output: one 8-15 minute authority script outline.
- Goal: deepen trust and drive offer conversion.

## Use Case 3 - Offer launch week

- Input: launch offer + CTA.
- Action: update profile, generate shorts + long-form around objections.
- Output: conversion-focused content sequence.
- Goal: increase qualified leads and sales calls.

## Use Case 4 - Team content ops

- Research owner: runs scanner.
- Strategy owner: generates plans.
- Production owner: records/edits/ships.
- Growth owner: tracks CTR, retention, conversion.

## Key rule

Do not pick topics from intuition only.
Always start from scanner evidence, then build plan, then produce.
